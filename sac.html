STUDENT ACTIVITY CENTRE

About Student Activity Centre

Our SAC aims to nurture leadership, creativity, and collaboration among students. From technical clubs to cultural fests, SAC provides a platform for holistic development and student engagement throughout the academic year.

Objectives of SAC

Community Building

Foster a strong sense of belonging and collaboration among students through various clubs and activities.

Leadership Skills

Provide opportunities for students to lead initiatives and learn essential management skills.

Showcase Talent

Encourage students to showcase their talents in cultural, technical, and literary events.

Student Well-being

Promote a healthy, balanced lifestyle through awareness drives, workshops, and support groups.

Our Mission

To foster creativity, leadership, and teamwork by encouraging students to participate in diverse activities, contributing to their overall development.

Our Vision

To be a vibrant and inclusive hub of student-led initiatives that nurtures talent and empowers individuals for future challenges.
